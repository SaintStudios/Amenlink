3. Data Collection on This Website

a) Server Log Files

When visiting our website, the hosting provider automatically collects information in so-called server log files. This data includes:

  • IP address (anonymized if applicable),
  • Browser type and version,
  • Operating system used,
  • Referrer URL,
  • Hostname of the accessing device,
  • Time of the server request.

Processing is carried out pursuant to Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR based on our legitimate interest in the secure and stable operation of the website. The data is automatically deleted regularly.

b) Cookies

Our website uses technically necessary cookies to ensure basic functions. The legal basis for this is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

We currently do not use analysis or marketing cookies. Should this change in the future, appropriate consent will be obtained via a cookie banner (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

c) Contact by Email

When you contact us at amenapphilfe@gmail.com, your information, including the contact data you provide, will be stored for the purpose of processing your request and in case of follow-up questions.

Data processing is carried out, depending on the context, pursuant to Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual or for contract performance) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in effective communication).

To optimize loading times, we use external Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for libraries such as SwiperJS and Font Awesome. This may result in the processing of your IP address by the following providers:

  • Font Awesome / Cloudflare

Our App offers a personal conversation feature with the AI model NEMA 2.5.
